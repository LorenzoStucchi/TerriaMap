Set out below is an excerpt of the report, enabling rapid interpretation of downloadable files.

Each text file consists of records each containing sequential order the following quantities:

a)   Year, month and day of the record in the format gg/mm/aaaa

b)    Start time interval of 10 minutes of reference of the measures in the format hh.mm.ss

c)    Average value of the wind speed in the basis range of 10’ (m/s)

d)   Maximum instantaneous wind speed which occurred in the basis range of 10’ (m/s)

e)    Minimum instantaneous wind speed which occurred in the basis range of 10’ (m/s)

f)     Standard deviation of the average value of point c (m/s)

g)   Average value of the wind direction in the basis range of 10’ (°)

h)   Standard deviation of the average value of point g (°).

The measurements of wind speed and direction are for samples of magnitudes made with a frequency of 1 Hz.

The unavailability and/or exclusion of measures considered erroneous or unreliable is indicated in the text file with the presence in the respective field of 9999 value.
