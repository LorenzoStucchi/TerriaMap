Anemometers data Stations 

Period 2000-2002

The files related to anemometrics data available in this section refer to the stations listed in Table 1 of the report A3-012640 drawn up within the Research System activities developed in the period 2000-2002 to the construction of the Italy Wind Atlas.

It is, in particular, files relating to a total of 32 measuring stations, whose location on the national territory, together with the reference period of the measured data, is provided in the aforementioned Table 1.

Access to data files of individual stations and their rescue is carried out using the link matched to the names of individual data files listed in the last column of the table below.

The files to which you have access through the link provided are currently in text format (.txt) compressed (zip).

The interpretation of the data must be conducted according to the information in the indicated report.
