About Aeolian

Aeolian is a WebGIS tool to support energy planning and has been developed to provide information on the general distribution of wind resources in Italy and to help identify areas where these resources may be of interest for energy exploitation.
It should be noted, however, that the contents of the Atlas cannot and do not intend to replace the detailed evaluation framework (micrositing) that anyone intending to build a wind farm for the production of electricity must always acquire from the candidate sites to qualify them from an anemological point of view.

All the data presented (even those derived from statistical data) are provided for study and research purposes and do not in any way replace the data produced by the appointed subjects (eg: GSE, ANEV).
